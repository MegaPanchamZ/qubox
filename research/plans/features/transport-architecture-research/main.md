# Transport Architecture Research

## Goal

Document how Sunshine and Moonlight work in practice, identify what makes them reliable, and translate those findings into concrete guidance for Qubox.

## Scope

- Host-side session, capture, encoding, and packetization design.
- Client-side receive, depacketization, jitter handling, and recovery logic.
- Input, scaling, controller, and platform integration concerns.
- Rust ecosystem options for mature building blocks.

## Deliverables

- Architecture research documents under `research/references/`.
- An implementation-oriented task list for follow-up work.
